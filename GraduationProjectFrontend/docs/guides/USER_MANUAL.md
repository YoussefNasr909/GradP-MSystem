## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Navigate to the registration page
2. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Student Code (for students)
   - Department
   - Password
3. Check "I agree to terms and conditions"
4. Click "Create Account"
5. Check your email for verification code
6. Enter the 6-digit OTP code
7. Click "Verify Email"

### Logging In

1. Go to login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"
4. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

### Password Reset

1. Click "Forgot Password?" on login page
2. Enter your email
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create new password
5. Log in with new password

## Dashboard Guide

### Student Dashboard

**Overview Tab:**
- View your statistics (tasks, XP, coins)
- See team progress
- Check upcoming deadlines
- View recent activity

**Gamification Tab:**
- Track your XP and level
- View achievements and progress
- Check global leaderboard
- See your rank

**My Team Tab:**
- View team members
- See team statistics
- Access quick actions
- Contact supervisors

### Team Leader Dashboard

Same as student plus:
- Assign tasks to members
- Review completed work
- Manage team invitations
- Track member performance

## FAQs

**Q: How do I earn XP?**
A: Complete tasks, submit deliverables, achieve milestones, and help teammates.

**Q: Can I change my team?**
A: Contact your supervisor or administrator for team changes.

**Q: How do I report a bug?**
A: Use the feedback form in settings or contact support.

**Q: Can I customize my dashboard?**
A: Yes! Go to Settings → Customization to change themes, colors, and layout.

**Q: How do I enable notifications?**
A: Go to Settings → Notifications and toggle your preferences.
